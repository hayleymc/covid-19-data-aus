COVID-19 update – 22 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported three new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight, bringing the State’s total to 665.

All three cases are returned overseas travellers, aged in their 30s and 40s. Two are West Australian residents and one is from interstate.

There are now six active cases being monitored.

To date, 650 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 758 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 735 were assessed and 728 swabbed.

There have been 402,667 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 69,135 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
