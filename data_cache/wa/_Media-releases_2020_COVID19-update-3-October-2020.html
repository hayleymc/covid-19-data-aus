03 October 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 3 October 2020

The Department of Health has reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The new confirmed case is a male in his 50s who travelled to Perth from Indonesia. He is in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total COVID-19 cases is now 686.

There are 20 active cases are being monitored and 657 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 881 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 863 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 425,925 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 72,989 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
