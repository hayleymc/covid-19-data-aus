26 April 2020

COVID-19 update – 26 April 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight, keeping the State’s total number of cases to 549.

There are 16 confirmed COVID-19 patients in Perth metropolitan hospitals – four of whom are in ICU.

To date 34,256 people have tested negative for COVID-19 in Western Australia. Of those, 6,115 people have returned negative tests in regional WA.

WA has a total of 486 recovered cases, leaving 55 active cases across the State.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19 and self-isolation.
