COVID-19 update – 14 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 659. There are four active cases being monitored.

To date, 646 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 482 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 477 were assessed and 475 swabbed.

There have been 384,601 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 66,286 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
