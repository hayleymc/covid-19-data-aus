COVID-19 update – 20 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight, leaving the State’s total at 651.

There are 20 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia, all are in hotel quarantine. To date, 622 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 477 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 464 were assessed and 459 were swabbed.

To date there have been 224,717 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 38,404 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
