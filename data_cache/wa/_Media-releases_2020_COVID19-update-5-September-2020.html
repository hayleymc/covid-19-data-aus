05 September 2020

COVID-19 update – 5 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 655.

WA has two active COVID-19 cases – both from interstate.

To date, 644 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 801 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 764 were assessed and 751 were swabbed.

There have been 358,331 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 61,748 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333
Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

NB: An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
