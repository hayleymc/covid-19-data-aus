COVID-19 update – 6 October 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total remains at 687.

There are 14 active cases being monitored and 664 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 807 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 790 were assessed and 788 swabbed.

There have been 434,333 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 74,242 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
