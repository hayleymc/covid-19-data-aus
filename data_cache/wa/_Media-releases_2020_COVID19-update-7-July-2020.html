COVID-19 update – 7 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight. The State’s total remains at 621.

There are 12 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia. To date, 600 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 663 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 620 were assessed and 615 were swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19. 

To date there have been 197,884 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 34,544 were from regional WA.
