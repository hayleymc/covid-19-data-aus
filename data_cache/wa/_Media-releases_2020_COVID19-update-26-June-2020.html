26 June 2020

COVID-19 update – 26 June 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight. The State’s total stands at 608.

There are four active cases in WA – all are in hotel quarantine.

A total of 595 people have recovered from COVID-19 in WA.  

Yesterday 768 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 739 were assessed and 733 were swabbed.

To date there have been 174,330 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 30,698 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
