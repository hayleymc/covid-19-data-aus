COVID-19 update – 19 June 2020

The Department of Health is reporting no new cases of COVID-19 overnight with the State’s total standing at 603.

There are currently two active cases in the State and 592 people have recovered from COVID-19 in WA.

Yesterday 737 people presented to COVID-19 clinics – 709 were assessed and 701 were swabbed.

To date there have been 155,815 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 27,336 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
