02 October 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 2 October 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 685.

There are now 20 active cases being monitored, following two recoveries overnight.

To date, 656 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 469 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 457 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 425,925 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 72,989 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
