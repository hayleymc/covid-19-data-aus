COVID-19 update – 15 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported eight new cases of COVID-19 overnight, bringing the State’s total to 644.

All of the eight new cases relate to returned overseas travellers who are in hotel quarantine.

There are now 29 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia. Of those cases twenty two are from WA and seven are from interstate.

To date, 606 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 753 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 725 were assessed and 715 were swabbed.

To date there have been 214,685 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 37,000 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19.
