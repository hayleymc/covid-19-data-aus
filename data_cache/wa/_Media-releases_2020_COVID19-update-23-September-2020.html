23 September 2020

COVID-19 update – 23 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total remains at 665. There are six active cases being monitored.

To date, 650 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 799 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 767 were assessed and 764 swabbed.

There have been 405,246 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 69,395 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
