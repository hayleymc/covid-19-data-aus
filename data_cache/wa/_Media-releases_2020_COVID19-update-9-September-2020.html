COVID-19 update – 9 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight. The State’s total remains at 658.

WA now has three active COVID-19 cases, following two recoveries overnight.

To date, 646 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 784 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 765 were assessed and 763 swabbed.

There have been 371,204 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 63,820 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
