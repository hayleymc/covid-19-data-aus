COVID-19 update – 30 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 684. There are 22 active cases being monitored.

To date, 653 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 909 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 869 were assessed and 864 swabbed.

There have been 418,773 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 71,837 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
