COVID-19 update – 11 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 659.

WA has four active COVID-19 cases – all are in hotel quarantine

To date, 646 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 523 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 512 were assessed and 510 swabbed.

There have been 377,283 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 64,824 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
