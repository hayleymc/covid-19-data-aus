COVID19 update – 13 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight, leaving the State’s total at 635.

There are 20 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia – all in hotel quarantine. Fifteen of those cases are from WA, and five are from interstate.

To date, 606 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 331 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 322 were assessed and 321 were swabbed.

To date there have been 209,072 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 36,281 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19.
