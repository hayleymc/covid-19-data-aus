COVID-19 update – 14 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported one new case of COVID-19 overnight, bringing the State’s total to 636.

The new case is a returned overseas traveller in hotel quarantine.

There are now 21 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia – all in hotel quarantine. Sixteen of those cases are from WA, and five are from interstate.

To date, 606 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 1,066 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 1,016 were assessed and 1,000 were swabbed.

To date there have been 209,567 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 36,386 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19.
