18 September 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 18 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported zero new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total remains at 661. There are three active cases being monitored.

To date, 649 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 507 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 495 were assessed and 491 swabbed.

There have been 395,402 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 67,964 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
