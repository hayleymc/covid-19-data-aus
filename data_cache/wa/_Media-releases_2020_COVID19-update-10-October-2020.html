10 October 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 10 October 2020

The Department of Health has reported zero new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total remains at 692 confirmed cases of COVID-19.

There are 13 active cases being monitored and 670 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 772 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 758 were assessed and 756 swabbed.

There have been 442,299 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 75,000- were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
