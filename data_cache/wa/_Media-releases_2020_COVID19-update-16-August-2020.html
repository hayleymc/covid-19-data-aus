COVID-19 update – 16 August 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight, leaving the State’s total at 646.

There are four active cases in WA.

To date, 633 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday, 420 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 407 were assessed within the clinics and 406 were swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19.

Note: Updated testing numbers will be made available on Monday.
