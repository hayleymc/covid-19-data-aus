COVID-19 update – 29 June 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight. The State’s total stands at 609.

There are four active cases in WA – all are in hotel quarantine.

A total of 596 people have recovered from COVID-19 in WA.

Yesterday 313 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 300 were assessed and 296 were swabbed.

To date there have been 179,326 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 31,806 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
