COVID-19 update – 1 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight. The State total remains at 611.

There are currently four active cases in WA. All four are quarantined in Perth hotels.

A total of 598 people have recovered from COVID-19 in WA.

Yesterday 690 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 657 were assessed and 652 were swabbed.

To date there have been 185,850 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 32,747 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
