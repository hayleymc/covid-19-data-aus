01 October 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 1 October 2020

The Department of Health has reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The new confirmed case is a male in his 30s who travelled to Perth from India. He is in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total COVID-19 cases is now 685.

There was one recovery overnight, 22 active cases are being monitored and 654 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 481 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 468 were assessed and 466 swabbed.

There have been 422,862 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 72,522 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
