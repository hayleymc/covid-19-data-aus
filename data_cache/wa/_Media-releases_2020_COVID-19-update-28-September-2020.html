28 September 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 28 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported zero new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

Test results for the remaining Patricia Oldendorff crew are still being processed. When the results are known, they will be reported.

The State’s total remains stands at 676. There are 14 active cases being monitored.

To date, 653 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 485 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 473 were assessed and 472 swabbed.

There have been 410,171 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 70,430 were from regional WA*.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19. 

*Note: Total test numbers will next be updated on Tuesday due to the long weekend.
