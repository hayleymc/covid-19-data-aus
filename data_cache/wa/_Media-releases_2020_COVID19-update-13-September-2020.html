13 September 2020

COVID-19 update – 13 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total case number stands at 659. There are four active cases being monitored.

To date, 646 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 415 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 405 were assessed and swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

NB: An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
