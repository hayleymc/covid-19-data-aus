COVID-19 update – 15 May 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight with the State’s total standing at 554.

There are seven active cases across the State with one in regional WA. The active cases are all Western Australians.

To date, 538 people have now recovered from the virus in WA and there are no confirmed COVID-19 patients in Perth hospitals.

Yesterday, 629 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics, and of these 580 were assessed and 572 were swabbed.

To date there have been 63,248 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those, 11,392 tests were carried out in regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
