COVID-19 update – 17 August 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 646.

WA has four active COVID-19 cases – all are Western Australians who have returned from overseas travel.

To date, 633 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 414 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 404 were assessed and 401 were swabbed.

There have been 291,398 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 48,507 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
