COVID-19 update – 10 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The confirmed case is a man in his 30s who returned to Perth from overseas. He is in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total COVID-19 case count is now 659. There are four active cases being monitored.

To date, 646 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 565 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 551 were assessed and 547 swabbed.

There have been 375,910 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 64,604 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
