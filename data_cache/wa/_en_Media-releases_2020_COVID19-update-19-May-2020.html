COVID-19 update – 19 May 2020

The Department of Health has again reported no new active cases of COVID-19 overnight with the State’s total remaining at 557.

There are three active cases across the State – one in regional WA.

To date, 545 people have recovered from the virus in WA and there is one confirmed COVID-19 patient in a Perth hospital.

Yesterday 674 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics, and of these 701 were assessed and 697 were swabbed.

To date there have been 67,649 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those, 12,086 tests were carried out in regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
