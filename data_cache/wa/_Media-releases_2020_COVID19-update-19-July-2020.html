COVID-19 update – 19 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight, leaving the State’s total at 651.

There are 24 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia – all in hotel quarantine.

To date, 618 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 518 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 496 were assessed and 493 were swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Note: Updated testing numbers will be made available on Monday
