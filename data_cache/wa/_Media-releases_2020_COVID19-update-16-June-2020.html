COVID-19 update – 16 June 2020

The Department of Health is reporting no new cases of COVID-19 with the State’s total standing at 602.

WA has just two active COVID-19 cases. To date, 591 people have recovered from COVID-19 in WA.  

Yesterday 1,082 people presented to COVID-19 clinics – 1,027 were assessed and 1,022 were swabbed.

To date there have been 143,418 COVID-19 tests performed in WA – 25,300 from regional WA.

(Note: Testing data is not available today due to technical issues. Today’s data will be reported tomorrow).

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
