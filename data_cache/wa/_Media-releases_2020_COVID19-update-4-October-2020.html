04 October 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 4 October 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 686. There are 17 active cases being monitored.

To date, 660 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 473 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 471 were assessed and 470 swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333
Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

NB: An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
