COVID-19 update – 12 August 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 642 and there is now one active case in WA.

To date, 632 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 722 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 692 were assessed and 688 were swabbed.

There have been 280,116 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 46,877 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19.
